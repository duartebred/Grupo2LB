Summary: The protein encoded by this gene belongs to the interalpha
trypsin inhibitor heavy chain (ITIH) family. Interalpha trypsin
inhibitor (ITI) is composed of two heavy chains (containing VWA
domain) and one light chain. The light chain confers the
protease-inhibitor function, while the heavy chains are involved in
mediating protein-protein interactions with the components of the
extracellular matrix. [provided by RefSeq, Sep 2009].
